# Day 1: Logic Gates & Universal Logic

## Key Concepts Mastered:
* **Basic Gates:** NOT, AND, OR.
* **Universal Gates:** Verified that NAND and NOR can implement any logic function.
* **Complex Logic:** Derived the XOR gate using the optimized 4-NAND gate structure.
* **Timing & Delays:** Studied propagation delay ($t_{pd}$) and how a Ring Oscillator generates a square wave ($T = 2 \cdot n \cdot t_{pd}$).

## Takeaway:
Understanding these fundamentals is essential for RTL design and ensuring timing closure in high-speed VLSI circuits.
